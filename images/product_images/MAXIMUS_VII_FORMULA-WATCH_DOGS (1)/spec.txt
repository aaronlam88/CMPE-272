Note
	1. The PCIe 2.0 x 16 slot (PCIEX4_3) shares bandwidth with SATAExpress_E1 connector. The default setting is in Auto mode that automatically optimizes the system bandwidth. If you install PCIe 2.0 x4 device, the system will automatically detect and disable SATAExpress_E1 connector.
	2. The mPCIe slot is on mPCIe Combo III card and pre-installed with a Wi-Fi/Bluetooth module.
	3. The functions support depends on the CPU types.
	4. These SATA ports are for data hard drives only. ATAPI devices are not supported.
	5. Support ASUS USB 3.0 Boost, UASP standard on the Intel native USB 3.0 is only supported under Windows® 8.1/8.
	6. 2 x USB2.0 port at mid-board shares with ROG extension (ROG_EXT) port.
	7. Supports G1/4" threaded fittings. Fittings require separate purchase.
	8. Chassis must conform to ATX specification for successful installation: brass stand-offs of at least 6mm are required.
